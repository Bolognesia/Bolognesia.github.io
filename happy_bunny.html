Discovery

For this project I created a brand focused in sell cruelty free products online.

"In Happy Bunny, our goal is to source and distribute into New Zealand, the highest quality, ethical products that meet the following requirements: Vegan, Environmentally friendly, Fairtrade & Sustainable. We proud ourself to only deal with ethical manufacturers and producers that share our vision of a Sustainable Planet and who are committed to partner with us for the long term to provide quality and affordable products."

Brief

As a first step I created a brand logo, style tile and some quick Low Fidelity and High Fidelity Wireframes to use as a guideline.

The app was created using HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Users should be able to:

  • Filter products based on their category
  • Display producs as cards or grids
  • Search products
