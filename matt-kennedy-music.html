Brief

The objective of Matt Kennedy's music and tutoring website is to showcase his extensive experience as a New Zealand musician and guitar/ukulele tutor based in Christchurch. The website aims to highlight Matt Kennedy's musician experience, and provide information about the music lessons and services he offers. The ultimate goal is to create an engaging and intuitive user experience that encourages visitors to contact Matt Kennedy, book lessons and get to know better the musical projects he is involved.
