Discovery

ECO, short for Environment and Conservation Organisations of Aotearoa New Zealand, is a non-profit organization comprising 50+ organizations across the country. Dedicated to environmental conservation, ECO undertakes various actions and campaigns while working collaboratively to advocate for improved government policies and better environmental management decisions..

Brief

Review and re-design the website of ECO to create public awareness of their cause, call to action joining the organisations and maintain engagement of existing members.

Methods

Use of surveys, open questonaries and iterations to users in order to decide low and high fidelity wireframes structure, prototypes and style tile. The detailed of the research can be found in the following link: ECO Website Documentation
